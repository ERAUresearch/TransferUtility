INFORMED CONSENT FORM

Subject Matter Expert Evaluation of Generative AI–Based Synthetic Airport Drone Detection Images for Dataset Refinement and Transfer Utility Assessment

Purpose of this Research: I am asking you to take part in a research project for the purpose of evaluating how domain experts assess synthetic airport drone-detection images generated through a structured prompting process. During this study, you will be asked to complete an online survey in which you review sampled prompt-image pairs and provide structured ratings and optional comments regarding prompt quality, image realism, operational relevance, and usefulness for dataset development. Completion of the survey will take approximately 90–120 minutes.

Risks or discomforts: The risks of participating in this study are no greater than what is experienced in daily life.

Benefits: While there are no direct benefits to you as a participant, your assistance in this research may help improve methods for developing and evaluating synthetic datasets for aviation-related applications.

Confidentiality of records: Your individual information will be protected in all data resulting from this study. Your responses to this survey will be treated as confidential. No personal information will be collected other than limited professional background descriptors. Any identifying information used for survey administration will be stored separately from survey responses. Survey data will be stored in a password-protected file on a password-protected computer accessible only to the research team. Information collected as part of this research will not be used or distributed for future research studies.

Contact: If you have any questions or would like additional information about this study, please contact Joel Samu at SAMUJ@my.erau.edu, or the faculty member overseeing this project, Dr. Chuyang Yang, at yangc11@erau.edu. For any concerns or questions as a participant in this research, contact the Institutional Review Board (IRB) at 386-226-7179 or via email teri.gabriel@erau.edu.

Voluntary Participation: Your participation in this study is completely voluntary. You may discontinue your participation at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled. Should you wish to discontinue the research at any time, no information collected will be used.
